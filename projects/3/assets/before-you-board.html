Port of Call

Home / Port of Call

Miami

Adventure awaits before you even set foot on the ship. While in Miami, indulge in its famous Cuban cuisine and coffee offerings, explore Wynwood’s vivid local art scene, stroll through the Renaissance-style Vizcaya estate, or check out all the hot spots that trendy South Beach has to offer.

Cococay

Next stop: Cococay! Whether you want to relax or get an adrenaline rush, it’s all at your fingertips here. For a low-key experience, take a guided nature walk around the island or spend your day on the beach. Thrill-seekers will find their match on the water, whether it’s by WaveRunner, parasail, kayak, or a snorkeling excursion with a certified dive instructor.

Nassau

Upon docking in Nassau, you’ll be greeted by the island’s vibrant culture, bustling straw markets, and picturesque Junkanoo Beach. Nearby are Paradise Island, home of the Atlantis Resort (and the Caribbean’s largest water park), and the private Pearl Island, where you can spend the day on the water—via trampolines, kayaks, and stand-up paddleboarding—or relaxing in front of its iconic lighthouse.
